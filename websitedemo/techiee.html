TECH BOOKS

"Clean Code: A Handbook of Agile Software Craftsmanship" by Robert C. Martin

"The professional programmer is constantly faced with a choice between two paths: the path of least resistance, and the path of professionalism. The first is easy, quick, and dirty. The second is hard, but results in code that is easy to maintain, extend, and scale."

Approx. $35 USD

"You Don't Know JS" (Book Series) by Kyle Simpson

"JavaScript is a powerful and dynamic language that can do so much more than just display web pages. But to master it, you need to understand it deeply. This series dives into the subtleties of JavaScript, explaining how it works behind the scenes."

Approx. $45 USD

"Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach" by Stuart Russell and Peter Norvig

"Artificial Intelligence is not just about building machines that think. It's about creating machines that act rationally in complex environments, learn from experience, and improve their performance over time."

"The Art of Computer Programming" by Donald E. Knuth

"Computer programming is an art, and like any art, it requires practice, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of both the craft and the tools at hand."

KNOWLEDGE PILL

Think and Grow Rich by Napoleon Hill

Whatever the mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve. This classic teaches that success comes from a burning desire, faith, and persistence, backed by a definite plan.

Price: Approx. 200400 (India), $5$10 (US)

The Psychology of Money by Morgan Housel

Doing well with money has little to do with how smart you are and a lot to do with how you behave. It explores the emotional side of money patience, risk, and long-term thinking.

Price: 300600 (India), $10$18 (US)

Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki

The poor and middle class work for money. The rich have money work for them. This central lesson of the book emphasizes that the wealthy think differently about money. They see it as a tool to generate more wealth, rather than something to spend. It suggests that understanding assets and liabilities is key to financial success.

India: 300600 USA: $10$20

The Lean Startup by Eric Ries

"Focus on being productive instead of busy." Tim Ferriss introduces the idea of lifestyle design and shows how you can build a business that works for you, rather than working for a paycheck. It also teaches how to automate and outsource tasks to free up more of your time.

Price: 400800 (India), $10$20 (US)
